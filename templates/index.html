How it works
1
Image Preprocessing
Resized to 224×224. Cast to float32 in [0,255]. Model-specific preprocess_input applied — no manual /255.
2
Transfer Learning Inference
Pretrained backbone extracts deep features. Custom classification head outputs a sigmoid score in [0, 1].
3
Confidence Scoring
Score ≥ 0.5 → Real · Score < 0.5 → AI-Generated. Results below 85% confidence are flagged uncertain.
